My experience

expand_circle_down

In January 2022 I started work as a digital marketing apprenticeship at huzzah! digital.

In December 2021 I completed the Digital Marketing stream of Creative Alliance's Digital Skills for Business bootcamp. The bootcamp consisted of weekly seminars on brand awareness, creating social media campaigns, data analytics and SEO.

In 2021 I also completed TDM's Get Ahead in Digital Transformation course, successfully passing Google Digital Garage's Fundamentals of Digital Marketing course, as well as MTA exams in Introduction to Programming with HTML and CSS, and Introduction to Programming with Java.

Between 2013 and 2015 I worked as an Event Safety Steward in central London. I worked at a variety of busy and prestigious events, from the NYE fireworks in Central London to the 2014 FA Cup Final, ensuring that visitors were well-looked after and potential trouble-makers were kept under control.

I worked for a few months as Marketing Assistant at Kids Run Free, a Warwickshire charity dedicated to keeping you children healthy and active. In my time there, I took on a variety of tasks, from bringing their website online to managing their social media accounts.

Previous to this I was also Projects Assistant for a year at Armonico Consort, another Warwickshire charity aiming to improve children's access to music. My role encompassed a range of duties, from the design and production of flyers and other promotional material to email marketing and copywriting.
